Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can I participate solo?

Yes, individual participation is allowed.

2. Do I need experience?

No, beginners are welcome!

3. What are the participation requirements?

You should have a laptop and stable internet access for virtual participation. On-site participants need to arrange their travel and accommodation.

4. Is there a participation fee?

Yes, there is a nominal participation fee that can be paid through the event registration portal.

5. What prizes are offered?

Cash prizes, sponsored products, and internship opportunities will be awarded to top teams.

6. How many people can be in a team?

Teams can have up to five members including team Leader.

7. Will meals be provided for on-site participants?

Yes, meals will be provided at the event venue for on-site participants.

8. What are the judging criteria?

Projects will be judged based on innovation, functionality, and relevance to the hackathon themes.
